ARE YOU IN CRISIS?

If you feel that you are in immediate danger to yourself or others, please call 911 or get to the closest Emergency Hospital.
If you are having thoughts of suicide please call:

The National Suicide Prevention Lifeline at 1-800-273-8255

The Crisis Text Line TEXT the word HOME to 741741

CALL 9-1-1

Go to the nearest Emergency Room
